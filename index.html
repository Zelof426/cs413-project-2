Project 2: The Intermediate Life of a Simple Cat

Instructions

You are a simple cat who has a craving for flowers. Eat as many as you can.

Press Enter to start playing.

Move with WASD.

Eat a flower by pressing E while standing on it.

Avoid the bee. It will speed up with proximity to you and for every 5th flower eaten.

Press R to restart.

Press C for Credits
